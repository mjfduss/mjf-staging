Production Center

Advanced production labs are available in Strong Hall and Craig Hall.

Strong Hall, room 111

This building has everything you need to make a polished film or shoot an award-winning news story.

Gear includes:

  • Sony FS7 (4K) and Canon C100mk2 digital film cameras with Zeiss prime lenses
  • Sony high definition broadcast cameras
  • ARRI tungsten and HMI lights
  • Kino Flo and Impact Fluorescent Lights
  • Sennheiser and Neumann mics
  • Zoom and Sound Devices digital audio field recorders
  • Panther Dolly with jib arm and Dana Dolly

Resources include:

  • A high-def TV studio that houses the set for our award-winning public affairs show, “Ozarks News Brief.”
  • A green screen and a living room set for cinematography work.
  • A recording studio where you can learn music-recording techniques and surround sound post-production in the recording studio.

Craig Hall

Home to our main offices, plus journalism and post-production labs.

Siceluff Hall

In room 328, you’ll find the new media lab. It has software used in multimedia and interactivity design work.

This lab has 22 Mac computers and a variety of mobile devices used for designing mobile apps.

Courses taught in this lab cover a variety of Adobe software, such as Dreamweaver, Flash and Photoshop.

Clay Hall

Home to Missouri State’s award-winning student newspaper, The Standard.

The Standard celebrated 100 years of excellence in 2012 and wins awards each year.

If you’re a journalism major, you can great work experience by working at The Standard.

The Standard publishes weekly and has a digital-first presence.
